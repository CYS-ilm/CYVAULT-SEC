INTERNSHIP PROGRAMS

Launch your career with real-world experience

CYVault offers two flagship internships — VATP (Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing) and SOC (Security Operations Center). Both are hands-on, mentor-led, and designed to build job-ready skills.

VATP INTERNSHIP

  • ✓ Hands-on web, API, network, and wireless testing
  • ✓ Work on live scoped engagements (with supervision)
